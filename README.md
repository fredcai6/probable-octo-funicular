A Mario-style HTML5 platformer where you control a pizza navigating through a world filled with turtles, teenagers, mutants, and ninjas!

### How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser
2. Use keyboard or touch controls to navigate
3. Collect pepperoni power-ups to refill your ammo
4. Defeat enemies and reach the end of the level!

### Controls

**Desktop:**
- **Arrow Keys / WASD**: Move left, right, and jump
- **SHIFT**: Charge (defeats turtles, gives speed boost)
- **F**: Shoot Pepperoni (limited ammo!)

**Mobile:**
- Touch controls automatically appear on mobile devices
- Left/Right buttons to move
- Jump, Charge, and Shoot buttons for actions

### Features

- **True Platformer Mechanics**: Jump on platforms, explore a large world

- **Limited Ammo**: Start with 10 pepperonis, collect power-ups to refill (max 20)
- **Power-Ups**: Pepperoni boxes scattered throughout the level
- **Enemy AI**: Enemies patrol areas, turn around at edges, and have unique behaviors

- **Lives System**: 3 lives with invulnerability frames after taking damage
- **Mobile-Friendly**: Touch controls for playing on phones and tablets

### Game Mechanics

- Move left and right to explore the world
- Jump on platforms to reach higher areas
- You have 3 lives and limited pepperoni ammo
- Pepperonis are precious - use them wisely on ninjas!
- Different enemies require different strategies to defeat
- Score points by defeating enemies and collecting power-ups
- Charge ability gives you a speed boost when moving right

### Tips

- Save your pepperonis for ninjas - they can only be defeated by shooting
- Use charge to plow through turtles
- Jump on teenagers from above for an easy kill
- Watch out for mutants - they're unpredictable!
- Collect every pepperoni power-up you see
